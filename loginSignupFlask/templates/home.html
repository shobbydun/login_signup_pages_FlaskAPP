User Authentication

To begin using our services, you need to either sign up for a new account or sign in if you already have one. Signing up is a quick process where you'll provide some basic information such as your name, email address, and a secure password. Once you've completed the registration, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. If you're already a registered user, simply sign in using your email address and password. This ensures that your personal data and preferences are securely accessed and allows you to seamlessly enjoy all the features and benefits we offer.
